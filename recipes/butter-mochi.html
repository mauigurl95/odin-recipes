Butter Mochi

Hawaiian Butter Mochi With Coconut Milk & Sweet Rice Flour

Made with sweet rice flour and rich, creamy coconut milk, this Hawaiian butter mochi recipe is the perfect blend of sweetness and chewiness that will bring the taste of Hawaii to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick unsalted butter melted
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 16 oz mochiko flour 1 box
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 can evaporated milk 12 oz
  • 1 can coconut milk 14 oz

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, and grease a 9Ã—13 inch pan.
  2. Mix the melted butter and sugar until combined.

    1 stick unsalted butter,2 cups granulated sugar

  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each.

    4 large eggs

  4. Stir in vanilla. Pour in the mochiko and add the baking powder. Stir until mostly combined.

    1 teaspoon vanilla,16 oz mochiko flour,2 teaspoon baking powder

  5. Stir in the evaporated milk, stir in the coconut milk.

    1 can evaporated milk,1 can coconut milk

  6. When batter is totally smooth, pour into pan and bake for one hour.
  7. Remove from oven and allow to cool to room temperature.
  8. Remove from pan and cut into pieces on a cutting board.
