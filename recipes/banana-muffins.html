Banana Muffins

Homemade Banana Muffins!

Start your day in the most delicious way: This banana muffin recipe is beloved by many because it's quick, delicious, and easy to customize.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking Soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • Optional mix-ins: chocolate chips, walnuts, pecans, oats, chia seeds, pumpkin seeds, dried fruit

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line cups with paper liners.
  3. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in one bowl; set aside.
  4. Mix bananas, sugar, egg, and melted butter in a separate large bowl until well combined.
  5. Fold the dry mixture into the wet mixture until smooth.
  6. Spoon the batter into prepared muffin tins, filling each 2/3 full.
  7. Bake the muffins in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, about 25 to 30 minutes.
  8. Cool briefly in the tin, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
